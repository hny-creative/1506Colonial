Our History

Built in the early 20th century, 1506 Colonial Ave stands as a testament to Norfolk's rich architectural heritage. This historic condominium building has been carefully preserved and modernized to meet contemporary living standards while maintaining its original charm.

In 2008, the building underwent a major renovation, breathing new life into its historic structure.

The Neighborhood

Located in the heart of Norfolk's Historic Ghent district, 1506 Colonial Ave is surrounded by beautiful architecture, local businesses, and cultural attractions. Ghent, established in the late 19th century, is one of Norfolk's most vibrant and historic neighborhoods, known for its tree-lined streets and diverse community.

Historic Ghent

Ghent was developed in the late 1800s as Norfolk's first suburb and is now listed on the National Register of Historic Places. The neighborhood features a mix of architectural styles including Queen Anne, Colonial Revival, and Arts and Crafts homes. Our building at 1506 Colonial Ave is part of this rich architectural heritage.
